Tom Yum Goong A Spicy Thai Delighht

Tom Goong is a classic Thai soup renowned for its bold flavors and aromatic spices. This hot and sour soup features a tantaliz

The key to a delicious Tom Yum Goong lies in the balance of flavors sweet, sour, salty, and spicy.

Ingredients

  • Lemongrass: 2 stalks
  • Thai basil: 1 cup
  • Kaffir Lime Leaves: 3 leaves
  • Shrimp: 500g5

    • Preparation

    1. Simer the broth for 10 minutes. This step is crucial for developing the flavors
    2. Add lemongras, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves. These aromatics are essential for the soup's fragrance
    3. stir in shrimp and cook until pink. Make sure not to overcook the shrimp
    4. Season with fish sauce, lime juice, and chill paste. This is where the flavor really comes together
    5. Garnish with thai basil and serve hot. Fresh herbs make all the diffrence
